Cat

 
This cat walks with four legs, the head tilts up and down, and the tail wags side to side. The building instructions also include two dog variations for the head design.

An autonomous program makes the cat walk forward until the distance sensor sees something, then look up and meow for attention, then purr and and wag its tail when you give it head scratches (you need a smart device connected to hear the sounds). You can also control all of the actions individually with the remote control program.

Cat Walk
This simple autonomous program makes the cat walk forward.
Cat Auto
This autonomous program makes the cat repeat the following actions:
1. Walk forward until the distance sensor sees something.
2. Stop, look up, and meow for attention.
3. Wait until the distance sensor sees something close (your hand giving it head scratches), then purr and wag the tail.
4. Wait for your hand to go away, then repeat.
You need a smart device connected to hear the sounds (which come through the device), but the rest of the program will still work if not connected.
Cat RC
This remote control program lets you control all of the cat's functions individually with 8 buttons on the remote: walk, run, meow, purr, look up, look straight, look down, wag tail.
